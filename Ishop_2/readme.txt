Based on the Ishop project, develop the Ishop2 project (online store) in the Ishop2 folder.
Two components must be described - "Store" and "Product".
One of the products can be made "selected" by clicking anywhere in the line. The line of the selected product is highlighted in color.
Each line with a product has a “delete” button.
By clicking the "delete" button, the user is asked to confirm the deletion (confirm) and the product is deleted.
